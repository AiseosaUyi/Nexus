# Scoring rubrics

## Opportunity fit score (0-100) — how much to prioritise a reply

- Budget/seriousness (0-30): real budget or clear scope beats "how much for a logo?"
- Fit to Aise's skills (0-25): product/UI, brand, web design, decks.
- Effort-to-reward (0-20): quick to win, worth the money.
- Client quality (0-15): established profile, reviews, real company.
- Warmth (0-10): they reached out / referred vs. cold.

70+ = reply first. 40-69 = reply, lower urgency. <40 = optional / templated.

## Post quality score (0-100) — predict before it goes out

- Hook (0-25): does the first line/frame stop the scroll?
- Visual craft (0-25): is the design/thumbnail portfolio-grade for THIS platform?
- Clarity of value (0-20): is it obvious what Aise made and why it's good?
- Fit to platform norms (0-15): format, length, tags match what wins here.
- Call to action / discoverability (0-15): tags, "open to work," link, next step.

Below 60, don't queue it — flag what to improve (usually hook or visual) and re-draft.

## Platform health score (0-100)

Average of: profile completeness, response latency (fast = high), posting cadence (last 14 days),
reply/engagement rate, portfolio freshness. Always report the ONE change that raises it most.
