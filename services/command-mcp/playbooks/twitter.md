# Twitter / X — playbook (kind: content)

Twitter is top-of-funnel visibility. Designers get inbound from the timeline: posting work,
build-in-public, replies. Also watch DMs/replies for real leads.

## Read (each run)
- Notifications → meaningful replies/quotes on your posts, and mentions.
- DMs → collab/job asks. Scam-filter (crypto/"promo" spam is heavy here).

## Draft (content engine, shared idea bank with LinkedIn)
- Post ideas: the design itself (image/video), a before→after, a short process thread, a hot take,
  a "open to work / taking 1-2 projects" note. Pull from the shared calendar.
- Reformat for Twitter: punchy first line, image/video front-loaded, 1-2 hashtags max, no wall of text.
- Reply drafts to legit inbound (type=message).

## What good looks like on Twitter
- Visual-first: the design/video is the post; text is the hook.
- First line stops the scroll; thread only if there's a real story.
- Consistent cadence beats occasional bursts.

## Health inputs
- Posting cadence (last 14 days), reply/engagement rate, profile "open to work" + link, follower trend.
  Top fix is usually: ship the design posts already in the calendar.

## Execute (after approval)
- Post: compose → attach approved media (Aise's exported design/video) → post → `cc_mark_posted` + URL.
- Reply: open thread → paste approved reply → post → `cc_mark_sent`.
- Human-paced; don't mass-reply.
