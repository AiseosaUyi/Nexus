⬡ The organism

It doesn't store thoughts.
It grows them.

Every node you add sends out hyphae — links to the parents and siblings around it. Folders branch into documents; documents branch into blocks. The structure isn't built, it's grown.

Drag a branch and the whole organism reshapes around it. Nothing is ever orphaned — in a living system, everything stays connected to the root.
