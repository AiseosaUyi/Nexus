One workspace, every kind of work.

01

Block editor

Headings, media, tasks, code, tables — every document is composed of blocks you can reorder instantly. Type “/” for anything.

02

Infinite nesting

Folders, documents and calendars are all nodes in one tree. Restructure the whole workspace with a single drag.

03

Real-time

Live cursors and instant updates over WebSocket subscriptions. Edit together without a single collision.

04

Notion import

Paste a URL — the DOM is parsed straight into clean Nexus blocks. h1→heading, p→paragraph, img→image.

Roles & access

Admin, editor, viewer — permissions scoped per business and enforced at the source.
